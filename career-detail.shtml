Senior VFX Compositor

Join our team and work on high-profile feature films and commercials

About the Role

We're seeking an experienced VFX Compositor to join our dynamic team. You'll work on high-profile feature films, commercials, and digital media projects, creating seamless visual effects that enhance storytelling and captivate audiences.

As a Senior Compositor, you'll collaborate with directors, VFX supervisors, and other artists to deliver world-class visual effects. You'll have the opportunity to work with cutting-edge technology and contribute to projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in visual effects.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create photorealistic composites by seamlessly integrating CGI elements with live-action footage
  • Perform color correction, grading, and matching to ensure consistency across shots
  • Work with 2D and 3D tracking data to integrate CG elements accurately
  • Collaborate with other departments to solve technical and creative challenges
  • Maintain shot continuity and quality while meeting project deadlines
  • Mentor junior compositors and share knowledge with the team
  • Stay updated with the latest compositing techniques and industry trends

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • 5+ years of professional experience in VFX compositing for feature films or commercials
  • Expert knowledge of Nuke or similar industry-standard compositing software
  • Strong understanding of color science, color grading, and color management workflows
  • Proficiency with keying, rotoscoping, tracking, and paint techniques
  • Experience with 3D renders, multipass workflows, and render layers
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills
  • Ability to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines

Nice to Have

  • Experience with After Effects, Flame, or other compositing tools
  • Knowledge of Python scripting for pipeline automation
  • Understanding of 3D software like Maya, Houdini, or Blender
  • Experience with HDR workflows and real-time rendering
  • Bachelor's degree in Visual Effects, Film, or related field
